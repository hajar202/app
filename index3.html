OPPO A92

Oppo A92 is officially released in May 2020. The smartphone has a big 6.5 inches display with IPS LCD capacitive touchscreen technology that provides 1080 x 2400 pixels resolution and 405 PPI density. The device is pack with 8 GB RAM options with 128 GB internal storage. The Oppo A92 runs on the Android 10.0 operating system with ColorOS 7.1 user interface and users are given various color options to choose such as Twilight Black, Shining White, and Aurora Purple. It features a quad-camera that consists of 48 MP (wide) + 8 MP (ultrawide) + 2 MP (macro) + 2 MP B/W and on the front there is 16 MP (wide) for clicking selfies. The smartphone is powered by Qualcomm SM6125 Snapdragon 665 processor. The device is fueled with a non-removable Li-Po 5000 mAh battery + Fast charging 18W. The smartphone is integrated with sensors such as Fingerprint (side-mounted), accelerometer, proximity, and compass.
